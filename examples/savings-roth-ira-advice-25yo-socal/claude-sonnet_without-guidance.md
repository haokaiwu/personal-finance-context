## 📈 Roth IRA Breakdown

**Why you should open one now:**
- 2024 contribution limit is **$7,000/year** (~$583/month)

**Where to open it:**

## 💳 Credit Card Strategy

### Should you cancel old cards?
**No — keep them open.** Here's why:

- Credit age is roughly **15% of your FICO score**
- Canceling reduces your **total available credit**, which raises utilization ratio
- A card with no annual fee costs you nothing to keep open
- Just make a small purchase every 6 months to keep them active

### One card vs. multiple cards
Honestly, **2-3 cards with clear purposes** beats consolidating into one:

| Card | Best Used For |
|------|--------------|
| Chase Sapphire Preferred (~$95/yr) | Travel, dining, sign-up bonus |
| Discover It Miles | Everyday/rotating categories |
| US Bank | Whatever it's optimized for |

### On the Chase Sapphire Question
At 780+ scores you'll **easily qualify.** Key considerations:

- Sign-up bonus is typically **60,000-75,000 points** (worth ~$750-900 in travel)
- The $95 annual fee pays for itself quickly with the bonus alone
- Chase Ultimate Rewards points are genuinely one of the best flexible rewards currencies
- **Good timing tip:** Apply when the bonus is elevated, not at its baseline
