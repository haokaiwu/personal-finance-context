# Data Points Reference

Quick reference of every required and recommended data point across the methodology.

---

## Per Category

### Universal (methodology-master-doc.md)

**Required:**

- Goal (what is the user trying to accomplish?)
- Age
- Partnership situation as related to sharing finances
- Annual income
- Total net worth

**Recommended:**

- Feelings about the situation (current and desired/expected future state)
- Amount saved each month
- Sources of debt
- Cash on hand
- Upcoming planned milestones
- Location for cost-of-living
- Retirement account and post-tax investment account values
- Investment allocation

---

### Career and Income

**Required:**

- Goal
- Annual income (incl. all income sources; household or individual depending on context)
- Cost-of-living based on location
- Satisfaction with current job
- Expected change in satisfaction with job/career change

**Recommended:**

- Sustainability of current job
- Career friction with loved ones
- Job title
- Job organizational context (government, startup, etc.)
- Partnership status
- Partner job title
- Partner annual income vs. user's
- Age
- Savings rate
- Total net worth
- Cash on hand
- Liquid net worth (bank accounts + post-tax investments - unsecured debts)
- Upcoming life milestones
- Number of years working

---

### Lifestyle and Spending

**Required:**

- Goal
- Post-tax income, expenses, and/or savings rate (at least 2 of the 3)
- Comfort with current lifestyle

**Recommended:**

- Total net worth
- Spending breakdown (housing, food, etc.)
- Cost-of-living based on location
- Cash on hand
- Upcoming milestones
- Partnership status
- Partner income and spending vs. yours
- Partner comfort with current lifestyle vs. yours
- Partner net worth vs. yours
- Spending anxiety (e.g. post-purchase guilt)
- Career satisfaction
- For specific "can I afford...?" questions: what, how much, when?
- Retirement plans
- Age

---

### Liquidity and Life Events

**Required:**

- Goal
- Liquid cash
- Event details (what is it, when, how much?)
- Savings per month (absolute value)

**Recommended:**

- Feelings about life event
- Total net worth
- Total asset allocation
- Willingness to be flexible with event plans
- Savings rate
- Partnership status
- Partner willingness to be flexible with event plans vs. yours
- Partner net worth vs. yours
- Partner income and spending vs. yours
- Other upcoming milestones
- Annual income
- Career satisfaction
- Cost-of-living based on location
- Retirement plans
- Age

---

### Retirement and Investing

**Required:**

- Goal
- Age
- Retirement plan (timeline, spending after retirement, full vs. partial retirement)
- Total net worth
- Total asset allocation

**Recommended:**

- Willingness to adjust retirement plan based on circumstances
- Annual income
- Monthly expenses and savings rate
- For investment guidance: what, how much, when?
- Career satisfaction
- Upcoming life milestones
- Partnership status
- Partner age
- Partner retirement plans
- Partner net worth vs. yours
- Partner income vs. yours
- Planned income gaps (continuing education, stay-at-home parent, sabbatical)
- Other investment goals (paying tuition for kids, etc.)
- Comfort with investing (risk tolerance, anxiety during market volatility)
- Tax bucket mix (401k vs. Roth vs. individual)

---

### General Status Check

**Required:**

- Goal
- Age
- Annual income
- Career satisfaction
- Savings rate
- Satisfaction with current lifestyle
- Liquid cash
- Total net worth

**Recommended:**

- Upcoming planned milestones
- Retirement plan (timeline, spending after retirement, full vs. partial retirement)
- Cost-of-living based on location
- Total asset allocation
- Spending breakdown (housing, food, etc.)
- Partnership status
- Partner income vs. yours
- Partner net worth vs. yours
- Partner lifestyle satisfaction vs. yours
