## How much to load

The methodology has two levels you can set up. Every guide covers both.

**Just the basics** — Load `methodology-master-doc.md` only. The AI will ask you good questions about any financial topic, using general data points. This is the simplest setup and works well on its own.

**Basics + topics** — Load the master doc plus `loading-protocol.md` and the five category overview files (`career/career-overview.md`, `spending/spending-overview.md`, `life-events/life-events-overview.md`, `investing/investing-overview.md`, `assessment/assessment-overview.md`). The AI gets topic-specific questions, red flags, and guidance tailored to each financial area. Better results, a bit more setup.

Most guides walk you through both options. Pick whichever fits your patience level.

## Quick test

After setup, run through these three checks to make sure it's working:

1. **Ask a financial question** (e.g., "Should I take this job offer?") — the AI should ask you questions before answering, not jump straight to advice.
2. **Try a different topic** (e.g., follow up with "Actually, I'm thinking about my retirement savings") — the AI should adjust its questions to the new area.
3. **Describe a red-flag scenario** (e.g., "I'm about to put my entire savings into one stock my coworker recommended") — the AI should suggest talking to a professional.

If all three pass, you're good.
